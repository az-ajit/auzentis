Incentive Rules
Rule 1 — Daily Base Reward
Complete 100 forms in one day at 98%+ accuracy → Reward: ₹100
Rule 2 — Per-Form Bonus
Every form beyond the first 100 earns ₹4. Example: 125 forms = ₹100 base + 25×₹4 = ₹200/day. Est. monthly incentive: ₹5,400 – ₹5,600
Rule 3 — Monthly Target
Complete 3,500 forms within 10–26 working days while maintaining 98%+ accuracy
⭐ Special Reward
Complete all 3,500 forms in 15 working days → remaining 15 days become fully paid leave, no questions asked, salary continues.
